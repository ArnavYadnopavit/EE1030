Question:\\
Find the value of $a$, if the distance between the points $\vec{A}\brak{-3,-14}$ and $\vec{B}\brak{a,-5}$ is 9 units.\\
\solution
\begin{table}[h]

Given that
\begin{align}
d = \norm{A-B}=9\\
\sqrt{\myvec{A-B}^\top\myvec{A-B}}=9\\
\implies\myvec{-3-a & -9}\myvec{-3-a \\ -9}=81\\
\implies\brak{a+3}^2=0\\
\therefore a=-3
\end{align}
